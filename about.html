About DAMYGASO TECH ACADEMY

DAMYGASO Tech Academy is a hybrid training institution based in Mwanza, Tanzania focused on empowering youth with practical digital skills for self-employment and innovation. Launch: January 2029.

Mission

To provide affordable, practical, and up-to-date tech training that enables students to build real-world solutions.

Vision

To empower young Africans with digital skills for self-employment and innovation.
